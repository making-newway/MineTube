# MineTube
Pytube and Tkinter based Youtube Video Downloading.

## Just use these simple steps to download your video anytime.
1. Add Your YouTube video Link and search. Check The Video Title if it is ok.
2. Opted your chosen quality and video format.
3. Change the Download Directory or simply press 'Browse'.
4. And Hit the Download button.
5. Voila, your Video is downloading. Just Follow me for a faster download! (;
